Optics & Imaging

Cheng Lab Boston University
Nonlinear Vibrational Imaging · 2025—Present

Developing two label-free chemical imaging systems with two postdocs: Vibrational Relaxation Energy Fluorescence (VREF) and Stimulated Raman Photothermal (SRP) microscopy. Work involves mid-IR generation via difference frequency generation (DFG), pump–probe optical alignment with picosecond lasers, and vibrational signal detection. Conducted spectroscopic validation on reference samples and applied data analysis in MATLAB and Python.
